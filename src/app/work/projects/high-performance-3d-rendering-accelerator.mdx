## Overview

Designed and implemented an ASIC-based 3D rendering accelerator for IC design
applications, completing the full flow from RTL to post-synthesis, place-and-route, and
post-layout simulation.

## Highlights

- **Throughput**: Capable of rendering 1,048,576-vertex meshes at 144 FPS.
- **Full ASIC flow**: RTL design through synthesis, place-and-route, and post-layout
  verification.
- **Hardware-software co-design**: A rendering pipeline mapped efficiently onto dedicated
  silicon.
