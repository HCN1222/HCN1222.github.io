## Overview

Reconstructs real-world car-accident scenes into accurate 3D meshes using Gaussian
Splatting, enabling precise inter-object distance measurement and dramatically reducing
public wait times at accident sites.

## Highlights

- **2D Gaussian disks for surfaces**: Replaced traditional 3D blobs with 2D Gaussian
  disks to improve mesh-reconstruction accuracy, yielding reconstructed distances within
  10% of ground truth.
- **Joint normal & environment-map training**: Extended backpropagation to jointly train
  surface normals and environment maps, reducing reflection-related artifacts in
  real-world car scenes.
- **Faster site clearance**: Enabled pre-clearance vehicle removal via advanced scene
  scanning, cutting public wait times at accident sites from over 30 minutes to under 10.
